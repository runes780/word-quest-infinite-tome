# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want Word Quest: Infinite Tome to be a respectful, practical, and educator-friendly open-source project. Contributors are expected to communicate with care, assume good intent, and focus criticism on ideas, code, documentation, and learning outcomes.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds in education, design, research, and engineering.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect privacy, especially around children and learning data.
- Avoid posting real student information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language
- Sexualized language or imagery
- Sharing private information without permission
- Publishing secrets, API keys, or identifiable student data
- Repeated disruption of project work or review discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate this code of conduct. Maintainers may also block participants who repeatedly act against the health and safety of the project.

## Scope

This code applies to project spaces including issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, and related community channels.
